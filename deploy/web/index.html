Features

GhostProtocol offers a comprehensive suite of secure communication tools designed to protect your privacy.

🔒

End-to-End Encryption

All messages, calls, and files are encrypted end-to-end, ensuring only you and your recipients can access them.

📞

Secure Voice & Video Calls

Make crystal-clear voice and video calls with the same end-to-end encryption that protects your messages.

👥

Group Chats

Create secure group chats with friends, family, or colleagues, with all the same privacy protections.

🖥️

Screen Sharing

Share your screen during calls for presentations, troubleshooting, or collaboration.

📁

Secure Vault

Store sensitive files, passwords, and notes in your encrypted vault, accessible only to you.

⏱️

Self-Destructing Messages

Set messages to automatically delete after they've been read or after a specified time period.
